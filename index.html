Why Jamaica?

Jamaica is one of my favorite travel destinations. Jamaica has great food, great culture and picturesque landscapes. I have been there multiple times but my favorite trip was to an all inclusive resort. 90% of the activities, food, and ameneties are included at all inclusive resorts as well as all tips. This means that your vacation is more easy to control financially. The staff at all inclusive resorts often go above and beyond to make sure you have the best stay possible. Jamaica has a happy, tropical, welcoming feel to it. Take a look at what it is like to spend a week in paradise!
